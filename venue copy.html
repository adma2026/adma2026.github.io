ADMA2025 Venues

Conference Venue

The 21st International Conference on Advanced Data Mining and Applications (ADMA2025) will be held at the Clock Tower Centennial Hall, Kyoto University.

  • Location: In front of the main gate of the Kyoto University Main Campus
  • Website: https://www.kyoto-u.ac.jp/en/about/profile/facilities/staff/clocktower




Welcome Party Venue

The Welcome Party will be held at the International Exchange Hall in the Centennial Clock Tower, Kyoto University.

  • Location: Floor 2 of the conference venue.
  • Website: https://www.kyoto-u.ac.jp/en/about/profile/facilities/staff/clocktower




Banquet Venue

The Banquet will be held at Hotel Okura Kyoto.

  • Address: 537-4 Ichinofunairi-cho, Nakagyo Ward, Kyoto City.
  • Website: https://www.hotel.kyoto.e.adw.hp.transer.com/okura/access/.
