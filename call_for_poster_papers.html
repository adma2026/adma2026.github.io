ADMA2026 Call for Poster Papers

The 22nd International Conference on Advanced Data Mining and Applications (ADMA2026) invites submissions of poster papers highlighting works-in-progress (WIP) or early-stage research projects. Poster submissions offer authors an opportunity to receive feedback, engage in discussions, and showcase ongoing research to the conference community.

Poster submissions should clearly outline the research motivation, problem definition, preliminary methods, and initial experimental outcomes or findings.

Themes and Topics

Poster papers should align with the following themes:

Data Mining Theories and Technologies

  • Foundations and algorithms
  • Big data mining
  • Data stream mining
  • Graph, spatial, temporal, text, multimedia mining
  • Web and social network mining
  • Recommender systems
  • Deep learning and generative models
  • Trustworthy data mining
  • Data security and privacy
  • Parallel and distributed mining
  • Visualization and interactive mining

Data Mining Applications

  • Edge intelligence
  • Bioinformatics
  • Healthcare informatics
  • Disaster prediction
  • Financial analysis
  • Education enhancement
  • Internet of Things
  • Smart cities
  • Cybersecurity


Submission Guidelines

  • Format: 4 pages (including references) using LNAI (Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence) format. Templates and formatting details are available in Springer's Author Instructions.

  • Submision Site: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ADMA2026.

  • Review Process: Single-blind review (author identities known to reviewers).

  • Proceedings: Poster papers will be NOT published in the conference proceedings.

  • Clearly define motivation, problem statement, preliminary methods, and initial results.

  • Papers must be original, unpublished, and in English.

  • Reduced registration fee compared to full papers.
